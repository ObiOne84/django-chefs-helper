About Chef's Helper

Discover the ultimate solution for your culinary adventures! Our application empowers you to create, store, and explore a world of recipes, all in one place. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook, our platform is designed to enhance your cooking experience.
Join our thriving culinary community today and elevate your cooking journey with our feature-rich, user-friendly application. It's not just a recipe app; it's your culinary companion. Start creating, exploring, and savoring the joy of cooking!

Cook delicious dishes easily and reliably.

On each recipe you can select the number of required portions from 1 to 10, the site will calculate the right amount of quantities you will need. You can search your favourite food by recipe name. Also, you can print your favourite recipes and save them as the PDF file.

You have a choice to either place on the public list and share your delicious food with all of us, or keep privately saved as draft and accessed via your free account. All your recipes will be added to your Recipes. Re-write and modify your own listed recipes.

Review, rate and like recipes.

At the bottom right of each recipe there is a review box, here you can leave a comment about the recipe. These comments will appear at the bottom of the recipe. The latest reviews will populate at the top. All comments are moderated for quality and relevance. You can rate and like recips to help other users to find theri perfect dish.

Weighing out ingredients

We highly recommend but is not compulsory to convert all ingredients into gram measurements wherever possible, you can do this with 2 sets of accurate electronic kitchen scales, one from 10k to 1g and a fine set of accurate scales to 0.01g, for 1/8 or 1/4 tsp measurements, this helps with consistency, ease and any future modifications. However, our application allows you to build your recipes with precision using various units such as grams (GM), kilograms (KG), milliliters (ML), liters (LTR), and units (UNIT).
